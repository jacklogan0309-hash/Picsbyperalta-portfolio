About

Sawyer Peralta

I started shooting high school football on the sideline with a borrowed 70-200 and never really stopped. Most weekends I'm at a field, a court, or a pool deck somewhere — waiting for the half-second that tells the whole story of the game.

Sports is still where most of the work lives, but I'm shooting more outside of it every season. New work goes up here as it's actually made.

Based in Overland Park. Available to travel for the right game.
